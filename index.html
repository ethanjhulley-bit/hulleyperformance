Two Ways to Work
With Ethan

Whether you're in Dubai or anywhere in the world, Hulley Performance has a pathway for your development.

01
1-to-1 Private Coaching
In-person private sessions in Dubai tailored entirely to you. Structured around your position, development areas and goals — covering technical, tactical, physical and psychological elements.
  • 45–90 minute sessions in Dubai
  • 1-to-1 or small group sessions available
  • Position-specific technical development
  • Tactical and psychological coaching
  • Personalised session plans each week
02
Online Mentorship Programme
A fully structured 7-module digital programme guiding footballers through every dimension of elite development. Self-paced with live mentorship support — accessible globally.
  • 7 comprehensive development modules
  • Technical, tactical & physical coaching
  • Psychological & nutritional guidance
  • Networking & media development
  • Live mentorship support included

UEFA C Coach · Founder & Mentor

Ethan Hulley is a UEFA C qualified coach who has spent 5+ years working with footballers across three continents — Canada, the UK, and the UAE. After playing for Leicester City's youth academy, going on a scholarship in Canada, and scoring 30+ goals from midfield in semi-professional football in the UK, Ethan relocated to Dubai to build something bigger.

"I started this because I want to give back to players — something I wish I had available as a youngster at Leicester. As a Christian, helping others aligns with my core beliefs. That's why it's my passion."

The Online Programme

7-Module Elite
Development System

A fully structured digital programme covering every dimension of what it takes to reach and sustain a professional level.

01
Foundation
Knowing Your Starting Point
Before you can reach your destination, you need to know exactly where you are. Self-assessment, goal setting and building your individual development roadmap.
Focus Areas
  • Player self-assessment framework
  • Goal setting & roadmap
  • Identifying strengths & gaps
02
Technical
Technical Development
Position-specific technical skills, ball mastery and movement — the non-negotiable foundation of elite performance.
Focus Areas
  • Position-specific drills
  • Ball mastery programmes
  • Movement & first touch
03
Tactical
Tactical Development
How the game is played at the professional level. Reading play, exploiting space, video analysis and positional awareness.
Focus Areas
  • Video analysis sessions
  • Positional awareness
  • Game model understanding
04
Physical
Physical Development
Professional S&C programming, injury prevention and recovery protocols built specifically for footballers.
Focus Areas
  • Strength & conditioning plans
  • Speed & agility work
  • Injury prevention routines
05
Mental
Psychological Development
Football is 80% mental. Confidence under pressure, resilience after setbacks, elite mindset and unshakeable self-belief.
Focus Areas
  • Confidence & self-talk
  • Pressure performance
  • Resilience building
06
Career
Networking Development
Talent gets you noticed — the right connections get you signed. Agents, contracts, trials and building your professional network inside the game.
Focus Areas
  • Working with agents
  • Trial preparation
  • Contract basics
07
Launch
Showcase & Launch
Preparation is everything. Highlight reels, personal brand, trial-day readiness and mapping your next career step. Now it's time to show the world what you've built.
Focus Areas
  • Highlight reel production
  • Social media for players
  • Career pathway mapping
